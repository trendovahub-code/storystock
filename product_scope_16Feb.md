# StoryStock Product Scope (16 Feb)

## Product Summary
StoryStock (by Trendova Hub) is a web-based stock analysis tool focused on helping users understand company fundamentals in plain language. It is designed for educational use, not for live trading decisions.

## Core Problem It Solves
Most retail tools present raw financial numbers without enough context. StoryStock combines structured company data, simple metric explanations, and AI-generated viewpoints so users can interpret fundamentals faster.

## What The Product Offers Today

### 1) Stock Search and Analysis Entry
- Search listed companies using a built-in symbol/company registry.
- Open a dedicated analysis page per stock.

### 2) Company Analysis Dashboard
- Top summary with:
- Price (clearly marked as not live), market cap, 52W high/low, book value, dividend yield.
- Quick facts such as revenue, profit, sales growth (5Y), dividend payout, promoter holding.

- Multi-section financial analysis:
- Growth, profitability, leverage, valuation, and quality metrics.
- Metric-level info buttons with plain-language explanations.

### 3) Financial Trend Charts + Interpretation
- Tabs for Revenue & Profit, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, and Efficiency Ratios.
- Each chart section includes a short “What this means” interpretation.
- Info buttons are available in chart sections for user education.

### 4) Fundamental Scorecard
- Simple pillar-based score display:
- Business Quality, Financial Safety, Valuation Comfort.
- Overall score and status label.

### 5) Benchmark and Ownership Context
- Benchmark comparison against sector averages for key metrics.
- Shareholding breakdown and key ratios summary.

### 6) AI Perspectives
- Three AI views:
- Analyst view
- Contrarian risk view
- Educator view
- Final synthesized verdict section.

### 7) Reporting
- Exportable PDF report from the analysis page.

### 8) Informational and Support Pages
- About page
- Markets overview page (sector pulse, market brief, watchlists, upcoming events)
- Disclaimer page
- Contact page (Name, Email, Message) with submissions logged to Google Sheets via backend webhook.

## Data, Reliability, and Compliance Notes
- The displayed stock price is **not live**; it reflects previous market-day data.
- The tool is intended for **education and interpretation**, not buy/sell recommendations.
- AI outputs include compliance-safe framing and are not investment advice.
- Data availability can vary by company; some fields may appear as N/A.

## Best-Fit Users
- Retail investors who want to understand business fundamentals before making decisions.
- Learners building confidence in reading financial statements and ratios.
- Users who prefer explained insights over raw, unstructured numbers.

## Current Boundaries (Not in Scope)
- Live tick-by-tick price feed.
- Broker integration or order execution.
- Personalized portfolio advisory.
- Guaranteed return signals or stock recommendations.
